Experience

How I got to where I am.

Unicity International

I started interning at Unicity International in December of 2021. It has been a great chance to gain some marketing experience and to see how an international brand utilizes their social medias, and how they approach new product conception. I have primarily done research and competitive products analysis, and hope to continue to grow my skillset.

UnStuQ App

In the fall of 2021 I won a competition to create a marketing campaign for the UnStuQ app, an app that helps people to make decisions in their lives like what to eat, what movie to watch, what to wear, things like that. I created the 'This Doesn't Matter' campaign, and was brought onto the team to implement it. I soon after left because I had a better opportunity that I came across, but it was great experienc to put a campaign into action on the social medias.
